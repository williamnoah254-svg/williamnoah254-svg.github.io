Get Involved

Every child deserves love, care, and the opportunity to dream. At Child of Mercy Children’s Home, we open our hearts to children who need hope—and we invite you to be part of that journey. Getting involved is more than an act of kindness—it is a commitment to building a brighter future. Whether you give your time, resources, or voice, you are helping a child feel seen, valued, and supported.

Volunteer

Share your time and heart. Help with tutoring, mentorship, games, or simply being present. Your presence brings joy, hope, and confidence to our children.

Why Your Involvement Matters

Every child deserves love, dignity, and opportunity. When you get involved, you help create a safe space where children can grow, learn, and dream again.

Many of our volunteers say this experience changes their lives too—through connection, purpose, and the joy of giving back.

We offer opportunities in teaching, caregiving, mentoring, and administrative support. Your skills and passion can make a real difference in the lives of our children.


Opportunities

  • Teaching & Homework Assistance
  • Mentorship Programs
  • Child Care Support
  • Event Organization
  • Administrative Support
  • Music and Arts Programs
  • Healthcare Services
  • Construction and Maintenance
  • Community Engagement
  • Sponsorship & Fundraising Initiatives

Time Commitment

Flexible schedules are available. Volunteers can commit weekly, monthly, or for specific projects depending on availability and interest. We welcome both short-term and long-term involvement.

Your involvement, no matter how big or small, creates a ripple effect of hope and change.

A Story of Hope

One child came to us shy and withdrawn. With time, care, and support from volunteers, they found confidence, improved in school, and began to dream again.

This is the difference you can make.
