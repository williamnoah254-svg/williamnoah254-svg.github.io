A Story of Hope, One Child at a Time

It begins quietly… with a child who has lost more than most could imagine.

Some arrive at our doorstep carrying nothing but fear and unanswered questions. No certainty. No place to call home. No one to lean on.

But here - at Child of Mercy Children's Home - something changes.

A hand is held. A tear is wiped away. A name is spoken with love.

Slowly, the fear begins to fade. Laughter returns. Confidence grows. And what once seemed like a broken beginning transforms into a future filled with promise.

Because of your support, over 50 children have found more than shelter - they have found family, education, healing, and a renewed sense of purpose.

We have watched children take their first steps into school, discover their talents, and dare to dream again. Each smile, each achievement, each moment of joy tells a story of transformation that words can barely capture.

This is not just impact. This is restoration. This is hope in action.

And the story is still being written… with every child who walks through our doors, and every kind heart that chooses to stand with us.
