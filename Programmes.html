Our Programmes

At Child of Mercy Children's Home, our programmes are designed to meet the full needs of every child. We provide care that goes beyond survival—focusing on healing, education, emotional support, and long-term development.

Each programme works together to restore dignity, build confidence, and create a hopeful future for every child we support.

Support a Child

Residential Care

Need: Many children come to us from difficult backgrounds — abandoned, neglected, or living in unsafe conditions. Without intervention, they face hunger, lack of education, and emotional trauma.

Solution:Our Residential Care program provides a stable home environment where children receive daily meals, healthcare, education support, and emotional care from dedicated caregivers.


What We Provide

  • Safe and stable living environment
  • Daily meals and basic needs
  • Emotional and psychological support
  • Spiritual and moral guidance

Impact

  • Safe & stable sanctuary for every child
  • Consistent care and protection
  • Holistic emotional and physical development

Education

Need: Limited or no access to education.

Solution: School fees, learning materials, and mentorship.


What We Provide

  • School fees and educational support
  • Books, uniforms, and learning materials
  • Tutoring and academic mentorship
  • Life skills and career guidance

Impact

  • Breaking the cycle of poverty
  • Improved academic performance
  • Confidence and leadership development
  • Skills for future independence

Counselling & Emotional Support

Need: Children facing trauma, neglect, or emotional distress.

Solution: Professional counselling, safe spaces, and emotional care.


What We Provide

  • One-on-one counselling sessions
  • Group therapy and peer support
  • Trauma-informed care
  • Emotional and mental health guidance

Impact

  • Emotional healing and stability
  • Improved self-esteem and confidence
  • Healthy coping mechanisms
  • Stronger relationships and social skills

Family Reunification

Need: Children separated from their families or lacking stable homes.

Solution: Safe reintegration with families and continuous support.


What We Provide

  • Family tracing and assessment
  • Reintegration planning and support
  • Family counselling sessions
  • Follow-up visits and monitoring

Impact

  • Restored family connections
  • Stable and loving home environments
  • Reduced institutional care
  • Long-term emotional security

Life Skills & Talent Development

Need: Lack of practical, creative, and social skills for independent living.

Solution: Holistic training that combines life skills, creativity, and talent development.


What We Provide

  • Communication and decision-making skills
  • Career guidance and job readiness
  • Music lessons (instruments, singing, and dancing)
  • Studio production and creative expression
  • Art and craft activities to nurture creativity

Impact

  • Independent and self-reliant young adults
  • Discovery and development of talents
  • Confidence through creative expression
  • Improved social and communication skills
  • Better preparation for real-world opportunities

Why Our Programmes Matter

Every programme we run works together to restore dignity, safety, and hope for children who have faced hardship. We believe that real change happens when care is holistic—addressing not just physical needs, but emotional and educational growth as well.

Many of the children we support come from backgrounds of neglect, poverty, or loss. Through structured care, consistency, and compassion, we help them rebuild trust, confidence, and a strong sense of belonging.

Education, shelter, and emotional support are not separate services—they are interconnected pathways that guide each child toward stability, independence, and a brighter future.

With continued support, we are not only meeting immediate needs—we are shaping responsible, confident, and empowered young individuals who can positively impact their communities.

Together, we are building futures filled with hope, dignity, and opportunity.
