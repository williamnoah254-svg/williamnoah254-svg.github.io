About Us

We provide care, education, and hope for vulnerable children.

Our Beginning

COMECH is a non-profit organization established on 18th August 2010, located near Shelly Beach and Likoni in Mombasa. It currently supports 24 children aged 3-19 from very difficult backgrounds.

Many of these children were orphans, abandoned, or living on the streets. Today, they have hope, education, and a safe home.

Founder Jesicca Opisa and her team provide not only essentials like food, clothing, and education — but also love, care, and a future.

The happiest moment for me is when I see the children. I know that there is always a light, which will shine for us. It is my biggest aim to offer the children here a good life with everything that they need.

“My past experience working at another orphanage fueled a desire in me to create my own children's home where I could implement all the ideas, practices, strategies and enrichment that I knew were essential to healthy childhood development.”

Jessy, Director of COMECH

Our Mission

Child of Mercy Children's Home strives to provide a better life to needy and orphaned children in Kenya. Our goal is to help them grow into self-reliant, fulfilled, and contributing members of society.



Our Goal

To address the needs of the children and formulate ways of attending to them by acquiring assistance in the form of donation gifts and support from charitable donor communities, charitable organizations both international and local, well-wishers, volunteers, local communities and government.

Our Main Objectives


  • Food Provision
  • Education
  • Infrastructure Development
  • Clothing
  • Medical Care
  • Love & Counseling
  • Agricultural Skills Training
  • Arts, Crafts & Life Skills

Food Provision

We ensure that every child receives nutritious meals daily to support their growth, health, and overall well-being.

Proper nutrition is essential for a child's physical and mental development. Our feeding program is designed to provide balanced meals that give children the energy they need to learn, play, and thrive.

We also work towards sustainable food solutions by involving children in small-scale farming activities, helping them understand the value of food and self-reliance.

Education

We provide access to quality education, school supplies, and mentorship programs to help children build a brighter future.

Education is a powerful tool for breaking the cycle of poverty. We partner with schools and educators to ensure every child has the opportunity to learn in a supportive environment.

Beyond academics, we encourage curiosity, critical thinking, and personal growth through mentorship and guidance.

Infrastructure Development

We work to improve housing, sanitation, and learning facilities to create a safe and supportive environment.

A secure and well-maintained environment allows children to feel safe and focus on their development. We invest in building and maintaining proper shelters, classrooms, and sanitation systems.

Our goal is to create a space that promotes dignity, comfort, and a sense of belonging for every child.

Clothing

We supply clean and appropriate clothing to ensure dignity and comfort for every child.

Clothing is not just a basic need—it is essential for confidence and self-esteem. We ensure that children have access to clothes suitable for school, play, and different weather conditions.

Through donations and community support, we strive to meet the clothing needs of every child in our care.

Medical Care

We provide regular health checkups, treatment, and medical support to ensure children stay healthy.

Access to healthcare is vital for a child's growth. We work with healthcare providers to ensure timely medical attention, vaccinations, and preventive care.

We also educate children on hygiene and healthy living practices to help them maintain their well-being.

Love & Counseling

We offer emotional support, counseling, and a nurturing environment where children feel loved and valued.

Many children come from difficult backgrounds, and emotional healing is just as important as physical care. Our counseling programs provide a safe space for children to express themselves.

We aim to build confidence, resilience, and a strong sense of self-worth through guidance and compassionate care.

Agricultural Skills Training

Children learn farming techniques to promote self-sustainability and practical life skills.

By engaging in agriculture, children gain hands-on experience in food production and environmental care.

These skills not only support the home's sustainability but also prepare the children with knowledge they can use in the future.

Arts, Crafts & Life Skills

We nurture creativity and teach valuable skills that empower children for independence.

Through arts and crafts, children express themselves, discover their talents, and build confidence.

We also provide life skills training such as communication, teamwork, and problem-solving to prepare them for real-world challenges.
